Amazon RDS

  • Managed Service: AWS handles patches, backups, and replication.
  • Scalability: Vertical (resize instance) and horizontal (read replicas).
  • High Availability: Multi-AZ deployments with automated failover.
  • Backups: Automated snapshots and manual backups with point-in-time recovery (PITR).
  • Monitoring: CloudWatch, Enhanced Monitoring, Performance Insights.
  • Security: IAM integration, KMS encryption, SSL, VPC connectivity.
